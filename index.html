ABOUT US

Passionately Crafted Ice Cream Since 1974

CreamKa, a well-known ice cream brand with manufacturing in Noida and catering to the Indian cities of Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, and Faridabad. Since our founding in 1974, we have worked tirelessly to perfect the art of making delicious ice cream by hand. Our dedication to quality and creativity has earned us a solid reputation. We combine classic recipes with innovative flavors to create a wide variety of frozen treats that are sure to please even the pickiest eaters.
